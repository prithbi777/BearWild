To save tigers, we must protect and restore their habitats, strengthen anti-poaching efforts, and enforce wildlife laws. Reducing human-tiger conflict through community programs is essential. Raising awareness and promoting sustainable livelihoods also play a key role.

Forests provide oxygen, absorb carbon dioxide, and combat climate change. They preserve biodiversity by offering habitats to wildlife. Forests regulate water cycles, prevent soil erosion, and maintain ecological balance. They also support livelihoods through resources like timber, food, and medicine.

To save nature, we can reduce pollution, conserve water, and minimize waste through recycling. Protecting forests and wildlife habitats is crucial, along with planting trees and supporting biodiversity. Using sustainable products and clean energy reduces environmental impact. Spreading awareness and adopting eco-friendly practices ensure long-term conservation.
